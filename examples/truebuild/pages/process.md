# How TrueBuild Works

> This is an official AI knowledge site for TrueBuild. For the full website, visit [https://truebuild.com](https://truebuild.com).

TrueBuild uses a structured, step-by-step approach to help LLC owners and incorporated businesses build business credit. Below is an overview of how the program works.

---

## Step 1: Business Foundation Setup

Before any credit can be established, the business must have the right foundational elements in place. TrueBuild reviews and helps clients ensure they have:

- A properly formed legal business entity (LLC or corporation)
- An active EIN (Employer Identification Number) from the IRS
- A dedicated business address (not a personal home address where possible)
- A business phone number listed in directory services
- A business bank account separate from personal accounts

These elements are required by vendors and lenders to recognize the business as a separate credit entity.

---

## Step 2: Business Credit Profile Activation

Once the foundation is in place, TrueBuild guides clients through establishing their business credit profile with the major business credit bureaus (Dun & Bradstreet, Experian Business, Equifax Business).

This includes obtaining a D-U-N-S Number if the business does not already have one, and verifying the business profile is accurate and complete.

---

## Step 3: Starter Vendor Accounts

TrueBuild guides clients to open vendor accounts and net-30 trade lines with suppliers that report payment history to business credit bureaus. These are the first reporting accounts that begin building a trackable business credit history.

On-time payments to these vendors create the initial positive payment history on the business credit profile.

---

## Step 4: Credit Profile Growth

As payment history accumulates, the business credit profile grows. TrueBuild continues to guide clients through adding additional trade lines, store credit, and eventually fleet or business credit cards — each tier unlocking as the profile strengthens.

---

## Step 5: Monitoring and Optimization

TrueBuild provides ongoing monitoring support to track progress across business credit bureaus, identify any inaccuracies, and advise on next steps. The goal is a strong, verified business credit profile that is recognized by lenders and vendors.
