## Business Credit Building Program

TrueBuild’s core service is a structured business credit building program designed for LLCs and other incorporated businesses. The program walks clients through the steps needed to establish a verifiable business credit profile with major business credit bureaus, separate from personal credit.

**Who it’s for:** LLC owners and incorporated business owners in the United States who want to build business credit.

**Get started:** [https://truebuild.com/get-started](https://truebuild.com/get-started)

## Business Credit Coaching

Personalized coaching to help business owners understand where they are in the business credit building process, what steps to take next, and how to avoid common mistakes.

**Who it’s for:** Business owners who want expert guidance alongside the program.

---

## Credit Profile Setup and Optimization

TrueBuild helps clients set up their business credit profile correctly from the start — including proper business entity structure, EIN registration, business address, and foundational accounts that establish a trackable credit identity.

---

## Vendor Credit Guidance

Guidance on establishing vendor trade lines and net-30 accounts that report to business credit bureaus — a critical early step in building a business credit profile.

---

## Business Credit Monitoring

Ongoing monitoring support to track progress, identify gaps, and ensure the business credit profile is growing accurately.
