## Who We Are

TrueBuild is a business credit building program that helps LLCs and other incorporated businesses establish and grow their business credit profile, separate from personal credit.

We work with LLC owners, small business owners, entrepreneurs, and anyone with an incorporated business who wants to build real business credit — structured, trackable, and independent of personal credit history.

## What We Do

TrueBuild guides businesses through the process of establishing a legitimate business credit profile. Our program is structured to help clients move through the key stages of business credit building: from foundational setup to vendor accounts to monitored growth.
